Cost Study Methodology

The Open Cost Study Exchange calculates instructional cost benchmarks using standardized program, faculty, student credit hour, and expenditure data.

Core Metrics

  • Cost per student credit hour
  • Student credit hours per faculty FTE
  • Organized class sections per faculty FTE
  • Percentiles by CIP and institution group

Public Reporting Rules

Public files should include only aggregated results. Raw institutional submissions should not be published without explicit permission.
