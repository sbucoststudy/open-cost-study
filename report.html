About this report

This tool generates a public benchmark report using the processed Open Cost Study database. Enter a CIP code and Carnegie classification. To calculate your department's percentile, also enter your department's instructional expenditures, faculty FTE, and student credit hours.

Higher percentiles mean higher costs relative to the selected comparison group.

Your department values

These fields are needed if you want the PDF to show your department's cost values and percentile.
